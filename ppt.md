## Slide 4: Problem Statement (The Pain Points)
**Heading:** Identifying Market Challenges
**Points:**
- **Manual Moderation:** Difficulty in tracking thousands of users and job posts.
- **Lack of Analytics:** Recruiters and Admins often work without real-time data.
- **Security Risks:** Public signups often lead to unauthorized administrative access.
- **Clunky Reports:** Exporting data for stakeholder reviews is usually tedious.

## Slide 5: The Hirrd Solution (Advanced Features)
**Heading:** Why Hirrd? (The Ultimate Solution)
**Points:**
- **Unified Admin Dashboard:** One-stop shop for all platform governance.
- **Real-time Analytics:** Live charts and growth metrics (No dummy data).
- **Secure Admin Portal:** Isolated login flow with no public registration for admins.
- **Professional Reporting:** Export-ready PDF reports for strategic analysis.

## Slide 6: Candidate & Recruiter Experience
**Heading:** Empowering the Users
**Points:**
- **Smart Search:** Location, salary, and skill-based job filtering.
- **Personalized Dashboards:** Track application status from 'Applied' to 'Hired'.
- **Integrated Rich Text Editor:** Professional job listings bina kisi external tool ke.
- **Vacancy Management:** Recruiters set kar sakte hain ki kitne candidates chahiye.
- **Hire & Fire Logic:** Ek baar hire hone par status lock ho jata hai, recruiter sirf 'Fire' kar sakta hai (Security and Integrity).
- **Auto-Close System:** Jitne candidates chahiye utne 'Hired' hone par job automatically close ho jati hai.
- **One-Click Apply:** Resume analysis aur quick application flow.

## Slide 9: Tech Stack (The Engine)
**Heading:** Modern & Robust Technologies
**Points:**

## Slide 10: Security & Architecture
**Heading:** Secure by Design
**Points:**
- **Isolated Admin Flow:** Admin registration is disabled; accounts are managed via DB.
- **Data Privacy:** RLS ensures users can only see what they are authorized to see.
- **Secure Storage:** Resume and Logo buckets protected with strict access policies.
- **Middleware Security:** Every route is guarded by auth and role-based checks.
